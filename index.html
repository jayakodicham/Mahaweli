Welcome to Mapping Mahaweli

Mapping Mahaweli is a website that features a digital map that archives the historical milestones of the Accelerated Mahaweli Development Project (AMDP) since 1961. I have marked dams constructed for hydro-electric generation as well as town centers established under the AMDP. I envision this website as a scholar endeavor which goes beyond merely documenting the mainstream historical information around the AMDP. In the Mahaweli Memories I have complied a collection of articles which narrates some memories shared by residents who were resettled in some colonization schemes around various outcomes, and social issues that emerged over the time as a result of this development project. I have combined, images, my illustrations, photographs and direct quotations from people in creating these stories. Where necessary I have also offered an analysis of what is in there. The subpage “About AMDP” offers a historical overview of the development project composed using various existing literature and archival data gathered from the University of Peradeniya Library and Department of National Archives in Colombo.
